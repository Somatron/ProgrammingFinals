Mediterranean cookery has been originated for hundreds of years. Originated by the people of the Mediterranean sea (Which comes from the name Mediterranean sea), spreaded through regions of Meghrebi, Egyptian, Levantine, Ottoman (Turkish), Italian, Greek, French, and in some places, Spanish. It consists of Olive, Wheat, and Grape, the three main elements. With additional sides of bread, pasta, and wine.
